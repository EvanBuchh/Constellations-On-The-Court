Jobs
  • If you take on a mission given by a faction, you may gain status with that faction depending on your current status with them and the number of jobs you've done for them since your status last changed.
  • Advancing from status -1 to 0: 1 job
  • Advancing from status 0 to 1: 1 job
  • Advancing from status 1 to 2: 2 jobs
  • Advancing from status 2 to 3: 3 jobs
War
  • When you're at war with any number of factions (status -3), the following penalties apply:
  • Your Crew suffers +1 Heat from jobs
  • PCs get only 1 downtime activity rather than 2
